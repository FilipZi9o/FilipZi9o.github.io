Black Gold Rush - Exploratory Data Analysis

Introduction

This was my final project for the data analytics bootcamp I did with Spiced Academy. It is an Exploratory Data Analysis of the vinyl records market with a focus on the electronic music genre sold on Discogs.

Roles

  • Data cleaning & Wrangling
  • Data Analytics
  • Data Visualisation

Tools

  • Python / Pandas
  • scikit-learn / Machine Learning
  • Plotly / Data Visualisation
  • Streamlit App

Project Duration

  • August 2024
  • 2 Weeks

Project Details

The goal of this project was to analyze the electronic music genre vinyl records market on Discogs, an online music database and marketplace. Going from the bird perspective of exploring the general trends in the music industry over the last 4 decades, especially the trends of sales of different formats over the years, and then zooming into the specifically the 1990's, widely regarded as the golden age of electronic music.

I analyzed statistical data from discogs to try and recognize trends, and relationships between different metrics and the prices of the records. 4 Datasets, found on statista.com and kaggle.com were used in this project.

Future steps

The goal is to evolve the current Streamlit app from reading data from CSV files to directly integrating with the Discogs API, enabling dynamic data extraction and real-time analysis. This upgrade will allow the app to perform statistical modeling and machine learning predictions on record prices, focusing on identifying trends in the electronic music genre. Ultimately, the app will provide users with valuable insights into potential investment opportunities within the vinyl record market.
