Company OKRs Futurespective Workshop

Introduction

Verimi is a German digital identity platform that provides secure online authentication, identity verification, and payment services for businesses and individuals across various industries.

The process of creating team OKRs and aligning them with the company's, has proven to be tricky for all the departments and teams inside the company. The workshop happened prior to the creation of company OKRs, that would happen on C level together with team leads of each department. And we tried to involve as many relevant departments as possible. The department that were involved in this particular workshop were business development, product and creative department.

Roles

  • Preparing and organizing the workshop
  • Moderating remote Workshop

Tools

  • Microsoft Teams
  • Figjam
  • Confluence

Project Duration

  • 12.22
  • 2 days

Workshop Details

Goal

As every employee and every department have their own unique view on where the company should be going, the scope of this workshop was to get valuable input about the participants' thought on company's immediate future that could then influence company's OKRs for the following year.
The goal of this workshop was to create OKR proposals that could be used in the OKR creation (which started shortly thereafter).
This was to be done by answering following questions:

  • What does our company whant to achieve?
  • How can we achieve that?
  • How are we going to measure the success?

Outcome

The expected outcome of this workshop was to have collected a mass of ideas and suggestions for Objectives and corresponding Key Results from the participants. No further actions were required.

Participants

As the workshop was intended to be a cross-department effort, business development coleagues were invited to provide the business strategy perspective, and the actual participants that joined in were:

  • Senior Corporate Development Manager (Strategy & Projects)
  • Senior Product Manager
  • Team Lead Product
  • Team Lead Product Design
  • Group Product Manager
  • Product Designer
  • Junior Product Manager

Methodology and Outline

This two-hour workshop imployed following methods:

  • Business Develompent presentation by the Senior Corporate Development Manager
  • Bad Idea Brainstorm https://www.sessionlab.com/methods/bad-idea-brainstorm
  • Individual Brainstorming & Group discussion Sessions

Conclusion and Screenshots

Conclusion

The intended goal of was reached. Although the number of participants was a bit lower than expected (a lot of them were ill and for some it was impossible to find a free slot), a good amount of ideas for company OKRs were collected, and the workshop was a productive ground for interesting cross-functional disscussions.
